About the Architect

Ar. Rita
Mody Joshi

"Architecture is not merely the art of construction; it is the art of creating environments that inspire and endure."

Founded in 1992, Rita Mody Joshi's practice has grown into a celebrated firm known for a nuanced blend of tradition and contemporary design. Based in Calangute, Goa, her work spans hospitality, residential, commercial and heritage revitalization — each project shaped by a deep sensitivity to place and client.

With a B.Arch from CEPT Ahmedabad, Rita brings both rigorous academic foundation and decades of hands-on expertise to every commission.
