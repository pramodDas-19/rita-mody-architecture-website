Biography

Shaping Goa's Built Environment for Over Two Decades

Ar. Rita Mody Joshi established her practice in 1992, bringing to Goa a rare combination of rigorous technical training and an intuitive sensitivity for place. Educated at CEPT Ahmedabad — one of India's foremost schools of architecture — she developed a design philosophy that honors local traditions while embracing contemporary needs.

Her firm works across architects, interior designers, and graphic specialists, focusing on execution and planning. Rita is known for understanding client needs deeply — an approach that has earned her an unwavering reputation for delivering spaces that are not just beautiful but genuinely livable and purposeful.

Beyond practice, Rita has contributed to architectural education as Visiting Faculty at Goa College of Architecture and through workshops at Manipal Institute, where she has presented design papers and mentored the next generation of architects.
